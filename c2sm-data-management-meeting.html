C2SM Data Management Meeting

Thu 05 March 2015
By Harald von Waldow

As suggested in the last Analysis of Climate Data Working Group meeting we organised an unformal get-together of data management concerned people from various workgroups. The topic was deliberately left very open, but talks revolved around persistent problems and solutions regarding access, storage and processing of large volumes of gridded data. No big solution to all problems was found, but it was nice to get to know a variety of different perspectives on the one hand side, and quite some common perceptions on the other hand.

A summary is on the web here: lab.c2sm.ethz.ch/data_management_2015.

Observe how nice my new disovery of presentation technology, reveal.js looks like. It’s quite limited with regard to layout and won’t make me throw away my LaTeX-BEAMER skillz anytime soon, but it is good for note-taking directly into a presentable form.
